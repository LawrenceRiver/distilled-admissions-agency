# Materials Bridge

Use this after program research, route cards, and scorecards exist. The goal is to translate fit research into application-material strategy without drafting essays by default.

## Inputs

- applicant passport
- route cards
- requirements
- public cases
- recommendation matrix

## Outputs

### SOP Proof Map

| Program | Route | Claim To Prove | Evidence | Weakness To Reframe |
|---|---|---|---|---|

### CV Emphasis Map

| Program Family | Experiences To Promote | Experiences To Compress | Missing Quantification |
|---|---|---|---|

### Recommender Proof Assignment

| Recommender | What They Can Prove | Best-Matched Programs | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|

### Portfolio / Supplemental Evidence Plan

| Program | Evidence Needed | Existing Asset | Upgrade |
|---|---|---|---|

## Rules

- Do not write full SOP prose unless the user explicitly asks.
- Do not invent achievements or polish away uncertainty.
- Use the route card language to decide what evidence matters.
- Connect every material recommendation to a program requirement or route hypothesis.
